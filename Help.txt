Goal of the game:
the goal of the game is to obtain a row of 3 
identical gems by swapping adjacent gems till 
desired combination is obtained.

levels:
the game originally strarts with 4 different 
colors of gems. with every 3 consecutive level 
another color is introduced.

Controls:
to swap gems of adjuscent rows, the user needs 
to click on the two gems he/she needs to swap.

Scoring:
For each match that is made, the user receives 
1 point added onto their score.

Levels are not implemented in this version.
